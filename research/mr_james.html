M. R. JAMES
Montague Rhodes James was an English medievalist scholar but is best known today for his four collections of ghost stories published in the early decades of the 20th century. These ghost stories reflected the antiquarian interests of James and were influential in the genre. Included on this page are examinations of his stories as well as writing nonspecific to any one story.
